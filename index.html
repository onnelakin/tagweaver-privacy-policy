TagWeaver Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the TagWeaver app.

Privacy Policy

TagWeaver values your privacy. This app operates as described below.

1. Collection and Use of Personal Information

TagWeaver does not collect any personal information.

  • No personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number
  • No account creation
  • No sign-in or login required

2. File Handling

  • MP3 files selected by the user are processed only on the device.
  • Files are never uploaded to external servers.
  • Tag editing is performed entirely on the user’s device.
  • TagWeaver does not collect, store, or analyze file contents.

3. Advertising and Analytics

TagWeaver does not use advertising SDKs, user behavior analytics tools, or third-party tracking tools.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

TagWeaver does not share personal information or file data with any third parties.

In-app purchases (Pro upgrade) are processed through the official store payment systems (Google Play or Apple App Store).

TagWeaver does not access or store payment information.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since TagWeaver does not collect personal information, there is no data to retain or delete.

6. Children’s Privacy

TagWeaver does not collect personal information from children under the age of 13 and is structured to operate without collecting personal data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to app updates or policy changes. Any changes will be announced through the app store listing or within the app.
